# AI-Driven Public Health Chatbot for Disease Awareness

## Project Overview

The AI-Driven Public Health Chatbot is an intelligent healthcare assistance system designed to provide disease awareness, symptom guidance, vaccination information, and public health alerts. The chatbot helps users access reliable health information quickly and efficiently.

## Problem Statement

Many people do not have immediate access to reliable healthcare information. Lack of awareness about diseases, symptoms, and preventive measures can lead to delayed diagnosis and health risks. This project aims to provide quick and accurate healthcare information through an AI-powered chatbot.

## Objectives

* Automate health information delivery
* Increase disease awareness
* Provide symptom guidance
* Share vaccination information
* Generate health-related insights
